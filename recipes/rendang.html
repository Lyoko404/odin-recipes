Rendang Recipes

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 kg beef
  • 1 liter thick coconut milk
  • 500 ml Frisian Flag Full Cream UHT Milk
  • 3 stalk lemongrass, bruised
  • 5 cm cinnamon stick
  • 1 nutmeg
  • 1.5 piece tamarind
  • 5 cloves
  • 5 cardamom pods
  • 1 flower bud
  • 1 turmeric leaf, tied in a knot
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 8 kaffir lime leaves

Spiece Taste

  • 100 grams curly chili peppers
  • 4 red chili peppers, seeded
  • 10 cloves garlic
  • 20 cloves shallots
  • 2 knuckles ginger
  • 3 knuckles galangal
  • 2 lemongrass stalk, white part only
  • 4 cm cinnamon stick
  • 2 tablespoon salt
  • 1 teaspon seasoning

Steps

  1. Boil the beef until cooked and tender. You can also boil the meat using a pressure cooker to cook it faster.
  2. Next, blend the rendang spice paste ingredients in a blender to make them easier to use.
  3. Saut√© the blended spices in a pot or pan over low heat until fragrant.
  4. Add lemongrass, cinnamon, nutmeg, tamarind, cloves, cardamom, star anise, turmeric leaves, bay leaves, and lime leaves.
  5. Add coconut milk and Frisian Flag Full Cream UHT milk.
  6. Cook until the coconut milk becomes thick and oily.
  7. Add the cooked beef until tender and cut into pieces according to taste.
  8. Cook over medium heat while stirring until the coconut milk sauce thickens or reduces.
  9. Check the taste of the Padang rendang, then remove from heat and serve.
