Sour Vegetable Soup Recipe

Description

Sayur asam (sour vegetable soup) or sayur asem (sour vegetable soup) is a typical Indonesian vegetable dish. There are many local variations of sayur asam, such as sayur asam Jakarta (Jakarta sour vegetable soup), sayur asam kangkung (water spinach sour vegetable soup), and sayur asam ikan asin (salted fish sour vegetable soup).

Below is the ingredient and steps to make delicious sour vegetable soup:

Ingredients

  • 1 bunch of long beans
  • 1 chayote (vegetable pear)
  • 250 grams long beans
  • 50 grams melinjo (Gnetum gnemon seeds)
  • 3 sweet corn cobs
  • 1.5 liters of water
  • 5 stalks of lemongrass
  • 1 tablespoon tamarind water
  • 4 cloves of garlic
  • 8 shallots
  • 3 cm galangal
  • 3 red chilies
  • 2 teaspoons granulated sugar (adjust to taste)
  • ¼ teaspoon salt (adjust to taste)

Steps

  1. Cut the long beans into 3–4 cm pieces or as desired. Dice the chayote, and slice the corn into rounds or according to your preference.
  2. Grind the spices until smooth. Once the vegetables and spices are ready, bring the water to a boil.
  3. After the water boils, add all the ground spices. Then add the peanuts, melinjo, and corn. Cook until the ingredients are half-cooked.
  4. Add the chayote and long beans. Season with salt and sugar to taste, then stir well. Cook until all the ingredients are tender and the flavors are well absorbed.
